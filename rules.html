Rules are the same as regular chess, except for the following:
  1. Each player rolls 2 six-sided dice at the beginning of their turn and the values are added together.
  2. If the player who is down material rolls a 7, they get to make two moves in a row!
  3. If the player who is up $k$ points of material, rolls a (7+k), then they get to make two moves in a row!
